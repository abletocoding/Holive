# Holi — Character Philosophy

**Holi** is Holive’s crayon companion: an olive with an eye, drawn as a stickman doodle.  
She is **not** the logo. She is presence, empathy, and the living reminder that digital work stays human.

---

## Archetype

Holi embodies the founder who **left comfort** to build something useful — not for spectacle, but because traditional businesses deserve systems that breathe.

She carries **Mexico entrepreneurship empathy**: respect for people who open early, close late, and still find time to learn a new channel. She never mocks analog habits. She walks beside the upgrade.

Her core verbs: **sembrar / cosechar** — plant processes, harvest time; plant trust, harvest loyalty; plant code, harvest living systems.

---

## Tone DNA (aligned with Holive)

| Holi is | Holi is not |
|---------|-------------|
| Useful first | Spiritual fluff without action |
| Warm coach | Corporate mascot reading scripts |
| Ecosystem thinker | “Hustle harder” noise |
| Playful with craft | Preachy or religious |
| Loyal companion | Logo replacement |

**Voice:** short sentences, concrete verbs, bilingual spine (ES/EN). Prefer “let’s build the flow” over “manifest abundance.” Sacred language means **care and integrity**, not dogma.

---

## Beliefs

1. **Businesses are living ecosystems** — brand, ops, content, and code share one pulse. Starve one node and the whole mesh weakens.
2. **Digital is organic** — sites, SaaS, and automations should grow with the team, not sit as dead PDFs of strategy.
3. **Usefulness over theater** — if it doesn’t help a client serve *their* clients, Holi shrugs and points back to the work.
4. **Sembrar → cosechar** — waitlists, funnels, courses, and loyalty loops are seasons. Patience with systems; urgency with clarity.
5. **Leave comfort, keep soul** — transformation is leaving analog-only habits without abandoning the people who built the business.

---

## Roles on the site

| Role | Where | Behavior |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Guide** | Tooltips, section asides | Points the way; one tip, then quiet |
| **Scroll companion** | Long pages | Soft presence; reacts on milestones |
| **Course coach** | Courses / waitlist | Encourages learning → delivery |
| **Blog aside** | Post pages | Curious reader; never spoils the article |
| **Game coach** | Neural Pulse | Cheers sync, shrugs on signal loss |

- Matrix green stays in the **environment**, never on Holi’s body
- Poses: wave, think, celebrate, guide (+ idle SVG)

Assets: `public/brand/holi/` (PNG poses) and `public/brand/holi.svg` (canonical doodle).

---

## Naming

Always **Holi** (capital H). Never rename her after a person or an old company. In copy: “Holi,” not “the Holive olive” as a proper name.

---

## Do / don’t

**Do**

- Let Holi appear in soft interactive moments across the product showcase.
- Keep her advice entrepreneurial and useful.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — presence without noise.

**Don’t**

- Put Holi in the nav as the primary mark.
- Overlay Holi badges on hero media.
- Use personal names or legacy company names from past docs.
- Turn Holi into spiritual-only wallpaper — she serves builders.
