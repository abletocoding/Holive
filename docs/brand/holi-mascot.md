# Holi — Brand Mascot

**Holi** is Holive’s crayon companion: an olive with an eye, drawn as a stickman doodle.

She is **not** the logo. She is delight, easter eggs, and the hidden minigame face.

---

## Character

| Trait | Spec |
|-------|------|
| Form | Olive body (oval), one large eye, stick arms & legs |
| Style | Crayon / hand-drawn doodle — wobbly strokes, imperfect fills |
| Halo | Small gold sacred arc above the head (optional in tiny sizes) |
| Eye | Gold iris ring + dark pupil; friendly, alert |
| Body fill | Pantone **2685 C** purple (`#330072`); never Matrix green |
| Halo / iris | Gold **871 C** screen (`#C9A84C`); bright alt **7406 C** (`#F1B500`) |
| Line | Rough **Black 6 C** (`#101820`) crayon stroke |

Personality: loyal, curious, a little mischievous — purity with play.

---

## On-brand rules

**Do**

- Keep crayon texture and stickman proportions.
- Use purple / gold / black / white only for Holi’s body and accents.
- Let Holi appear in soft moments: footer easter egg, game, empty states, stickers.

**Don’t**

- Clean her up into a flat corporate vector mascot that replaces the logo.
- Put Holi in the nav as the primary mark.
- Add speech bubbles, badges, or promo stickers on hero media.
- Recolor Holi in neon green for “Matrix mode” — green stays in the environment, not on Holi.

## Poses (for later game / motion)

1. **Idle** — slight sway, blink.
2. **Run** — stick legs cycle.
3. **Collect** — arms up, gold spark (brand gold, not green).
4. **Celebrate** — halo spins once.

Keep motion intentional and sparse; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

---

## Naming

Always **Holi** (one word, capital H). In ES/EN copy: “Holi,” never “the Holive olive” as her proper name.
