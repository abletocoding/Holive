# Holive Brand Brief

**Domain:** [holive.org](https://holive.org)  
**Name:** Holive (H-O-L-I-V-E)

---

## Naming

**Holive** is a portmanteau of three ideas spoken as one word:

| Root | Meaning in brand |
|------|------------------|
| **Holy** | Purity, sacred intent, trust earned — work done with care and integrity |
| **Olive** | Loyalty, peace, endurance; the living mark of the brand (fruit + eye) |
| **Live** | Presence, motion, systems that run; digital life in real time |

Spelling is always **Holive** (capital H). In all-caps lockups: **HOLIVE**. Never “HoLive,” “Holiv,” or “Holyve.”

Spoken: *HO-live* (two syllables; stress on the first).

---

## Promise

Holive helps traditional businesses and growth-minded teams move into digital life without losing soul — marketing, systems, and learning that feel pure, loyal, and alive.

---

## Personality & tone

| We are | We are not |
|--------|------------|
| Clear, loyal, precise | Cold corporate jargon |
| Sacred-serious about craft | Preachy or religious |
| Code-aware, Matrix-curious | Neon cyberpunk cliché |
| Warm when human, sharp when technical | Generic “agency vibes” |

**Voice cues**

- Short sentences. Concrete verbs.
- Spanish and English share the same spine: purity + loyalty + live systems.
- Matrix / code language is atmospheric (rain of glyphs, mono layers), not the product pitch.
- Prefer “we build / we digitize / we teach” over “synergy / disruption / 360°.”

---

## Concept pillars

1. **Purity** — Honest offers, clean design, no clutter in the first impression.
2. **Loyalty** — Long relationships; olive as symbol of lasting partnership.
3. **Live / code** — Digital presence that runs: sites, SaaS, automations, courses that keep moving.

---

## Audience (brand lens)

- SMBs leaving analog-only habits for digital.
- Teams that need marketing + systems, not just ads.
- Learners who want courses that upsell into real delivery.

---

## Visual anchors (summary)

- **Palette:** purple (**Pantone 2685 C**), white, gold (**871 C** metallic / **7406 C** digital alt), black (**Black 6 C**) — see `color-tokens.md`.
- **Logo:** olive + eye + sacred halo in Holi’s crayon/doodle style (see `visual-identity.md`).
- **Mascot:** Holi — crayon olive stickman (see `holi-mascot.md`).
- **Matrix green:** effect accent only — never primary brand color.

---

## Do / don’t

**Do**

- Lead with the Holive name as a hero-level signal on branded surfaces.
- Use gold for sacred highlights and CTAs of high trust.
- Keep Matrix rain / code as motion and texture, not logo fill.

**Don’t**

- Replace purple/gold with Matrix green as the brand identity.
- Use Inter, Roboto, Arial, or default system stacks as brand fonts.
- Treat Holi as a corporate icon — she is a doodle companion, not the logo (the logo shares her crayon energy but is olive + eye + halo only).
